Parking

Street parking along E. Moreno and free public lots one block east. No dedicated lot — come on foot from downtown if you can.

Dogs Welcome

Leashed, good-natured, water-bowl-enjoying dogs are part of the vibe in the outdoor beer garden.

Kids Too

Root beer on tap, a board-game shelf, and patio seating make this a full-family stop during daytime hours.

Food

No kitchen on site — but Italian Pie is right next door and delivers to the taproom. Outside food is always welcome too.
