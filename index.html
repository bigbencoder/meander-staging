About Meander

Two Decades. One Taproom.

Meander Brewing Co. has been a dream of the two owners, Mike Branam and Paul Bushell, for almost two decades.

From countless awards at beer festivals to late nights in each other's home bars, the two have had a clear goal: make some of the best craft beer around. Using the freshest ingredients, Meander offers incredible craft beers for the craft beer drinker and the casual light beer drinker alike.
